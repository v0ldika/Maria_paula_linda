ABOUT LYNN

Street artist specializing in vibrant, kawaii-inspired characters and bold graffiti murals. Based in Brazil, LYNN transforms urban landscapes with colorful narratives that blend pop culture, anime aesthetics, and street art tradition.

Working with spray paint, acrylics, and mixed media, each piece brings joy and color to public spaces, creating immersive experiences that connect with diverse audiences.
